Our Process

Methodical. Measured. 100% detail driven.

Every vehicle is inspected under dedicated lighting, with documentation of paint condition and realistic expectations before any work begins.

Written recommendations tailored to how you drive & store.
Clear communication before, during, and after your appointment.
Step 01 Consult & Inspect

Vehicle Assessment

Lighting-aided inspection of paint, wheels, and interior to understand current condition, owner concerns, and your goals.

  • Document paint imperfections and interior condition
  • Discuss owner priorities and expectations
  • Provide preliminary recommendations
Step 02 Safe Prep

Decontamination & Wash

Pre-wash, contact wash, iron removal, claying, and taping to prep every surface for defect removal.

  • PH-balanced pre-wash to protect paint
  • Iron fallout removal and claying
  • Surface taping and masking as needed
Step 03 Refine

Correction & Finishing

Machine polishing, spot compounding, and finishing tailored to your paint system.

  • Progressive machine polishing steps
  • Spot correction for deep scratches
  • Finishing polish for clarity & gloss
Step 04 Protect

Coating & Aftercare

Premium protection applied to paint, wheels, and glass, followed by education to maintain results.

  • High-quality coating for long-term durability
  • Glass and wheel protection
  • Aftercare guidance & maintenance tips

About

Built for people who truly care about their cars.

Award-winning Best Detailing & Best Window Tint — Folsom & El Dorado Hills

Detail Driven was created for the community to provide a trusted, reputable expert in the automotive restyling industry. For decades the auto industry has sometimes taken advantage of customers who don't always understand the technical side of their needs — we focus on honesty and integrity so our clients can feel confident leaving their vehicles in our care.

Our niche is the luxury side of the automotive world. These services are often a "want" rather than a "need," and we cherish the opportunity our clients entrust us with. Enhancing a vehicle — many people’s second-largest investment after their home — is work we take pride in.

We chose to build Detail Driven in El Dorado Hills so clients can rest assured their vehicles are safe: the studio is fully insured and licensed, with 24/7 video security. We’ve served collections, corporate fleets, and specialty vehicles valued over $3M — but we welcome anyone who needs professional, careful automotive care.
